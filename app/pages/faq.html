What is IV Vitamin Therapy?
Research shows that intravenous consumption of vitamins is much more powerful than oral use. Vitamins delivered directly to the bloodstream via IV are 100% bioavailable, compared to 20%-30% via pill form.
Are the staff trained/licensed to be giving IV services?
Yes! All staff that will perform this service have all of the necessary qualifications to be performing IV services.

We are unable to offer this service experiencing Kidney Disease, and expectant mothers. Otherwise, anyone over 18 is eligible to set up a consultation. At the consultation, we will screen you for any other possible prohibitive conditions.
Why Choose Medical Marijuana?
Medical marijuana is a term for derivatives of the cannabis sativa plant that are used to ease symptoms caused by certain medical conditions. Cannabis sativa contains many active compounds, of which THC, and CBD are the most well known. There are many studies still ongoing to assess the effectiveness of Marijuana for specific conditions, but some of the commonly accepted benefits include - Pain Management, Reduced Inflammation, benefits in treating neurological and mental disorders, and sleep management. After meeting with our clinicians, we will assess your specific medical needs, listen to your concerns, and determine if medical marijuana is the right treatment for your condition.
